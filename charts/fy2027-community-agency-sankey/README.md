# FY2027 Community Agency Allocations Sankey

Interactive Sankey visualization of Berkeley FY2027 community agency allocations by funding source, category, and agency.

## Source

City of Berkeley, Supplemental Packet for June 23, 2026, Item #1, Adoption of FY27-FY28 Budget and FY27-FY31 Capital Improvement Program.

Source PDF: https://berkeleyca.gov/sites/default/files/2026-06/Supp%203%20Packet%20-%206.23.26%20%231%20Adoption%20of%20FY%2027-FY28%20%26%20FY27-FY31%20CIP-Rev%20Agenda%20Material%20Cover.pdf

## Data

The extracted data is in `data.csv`.

Total visualized allocation: $21,460,545.

## Notes

The visualization shows:

1. Funding source to category
2. Category to agency

Hover or click a node or flow to view totals and percentages.
